## ðŸ‘¥ Primary User Persona: Commercial Cleaning Operations Manager

### Demographics
- **Age range:** 35-55
- **Job title/role:** Operations Manager, Owner/Operator, Account Manager
- **Industry:** Commercial janitorial services
- **Company size:** 10-100 cleaners, $500k-$5M annual revenue
- **Tech savviness:** Medium (uses Excel, QuickBooks, basic software)
- **Geographic:** Mid-sized cities (100k-2M population)

### Pain Points
1. **Contract risk from QA disputes** - "Client says it wasn't cleaned, we have no proof"
2. **Chargebacks eating into margins** - "We lose $500-2k/month to disputed work"
3. **Manual scheduling chaos** - "I spend 2 hours every morning dealing with no-shows"
4. **No crew accountability** - "I don't know who actually cleaned what site"
5. **Dispatcher burnout** - "Call-outs and rescheduling are killing my office manager"
6. **Client communication overhead** - "Clients constantly ask for status updates"

### Goals
1. **Protect contracts** - Never lose a contract over QA disputes
2. **Reduce chargebacks** - Cut disputed charges by 50%+
3. **Save time** - Get 2+ hours back from scheduling/admin
4. **Improve accountability** - Know exactly who did what, when
5. **Scale operations** - Add more sites without adding office staff
6. **Impress clients** - Offer real-time visibility they don't expect

### How They'll Use This Product

**Morning (6am-8am):**
- Check overnight shifts for exceptions/no-shows
- Review crew check-ins via geofencing
- Handle client inquiries with QA dashboard links

**Daytime (8am-5pm):**
- Drag-drop schedule adjustments for call-outs
- Monitor live exceptions and escalate issues
- Share proof-of-work photos with clients

**Evening (5pm-8pm):**
- Review completed shifts and photo verification
- Approve timesheets with geofence data
- Plan next day's assignments

**Weekly:**
- Review analytics (attendance, exceptions, revenue/hour)
- Export data for invoicing/QuickBooks
- Check client QA satisfaction

### Buying Process
1. **Trigger:** Lost contract, client complaint, or dispatcher burnout
2. **Research:** Google search, industry Facebook groups, peer recommendations
3. **Evaluation:** Demo must show proof-of-work and time savings
4. **Decision:** Will pay if it saves 2+ hours/day or reduces chargebacks
5. **Budget:** $200-500/month is acceptable for clear ROI
6. **Timeline:** Can implement within 1-2 weeks (urgent pain)

## ðŸ‘¥ Secondary User Persona: Cleaning Crew Lead

### Demographics
- **Age range:** 25-50
- **Job title/role:** Crew Lead, Team Supervisor
- **Industry:** Commercial cleaning (frontline worker)
- **Education:** High school or equivalent
- **Tech savviness:** Low to medium (uses smartphone for personal use)
- **Language:** English or Spanish (often ESL)

### Pain Points
1. **Time disputes** - "Boss says I clocked in late, but I was on time"
2. **Unclear expectations** - "I don't know exactly what to clean at each site"
3. **Blame for issues** - "Client complains but I did the work"
4. **Communication barriers** - "I don't understand written instructions"
5. **No proof of work** - "I did everything, but client says I didn't"

### Goals
1. **Clear expectations** - Know exactly what to clean (checklists)
2. **Fair time tracking** - Accurate clock in/out (no disputes)
3. **Protect reputation** - Proof I did the work (photos)
4. **Easy communication** - Report issues quickly (in my language)
5. **Avoid blame** - Show evidence when questioned

### How They'll Use This Product

**Arrival at site (5am-9am):**
- Clock in with geofence auto-detect
- Open site checklist on mobile
- Review special notes (keys, access codes)

**During cleaning (2-4 hours):**
- Follow checklist task-by-task
- Take photos of completed areas
- Report issues (broken equipment, supply shortage)

**Departure from site:**
- Mark checklist complete
- Submit photos (3-5 per site)
- Clock out with geofence
- Move to next site

**End of day:**
- Review completed shifts
- Check for notes from supervisor

### Success Metrics for Crews
- **Faster check-in** (< 30 seconds vs. paper sign-in)
- **Clear tasks** (no guessing what to clean)
- **Fair time tracking** (geofence prevents disputes)
- **Less blame** (photos prove work done)

2. **Proof of work** - Photos/video of completed tasks
3. **Self-service** - Check status without calling/emailing
4. **Issue reporting** - Report problems directly to cleaning company
5. **Easy billing** - Verify charges match work completed

### How They'll Use This Product (Client QA Dashboard)

**Weekly (Monday mornings):**
- Review last week's cleaning schedule
- Check all sites were cleaned on time
- View photo proof of completed work

**As needed:**
- Check if today's cleaning is complete
- View specific area photos (restrooms, lobbies)
- Report issues with photo evidence
- Verify special requests were completed

**Monthly:**
- Review invoices vs. completed work
- Export QA reports for management
- Rate cleaning quality

### Willingness to Pay
- **Cleaning company pays** - $49/site/month for QA dashboard access
- **High perceived value** - Visibility alone worth 10% premium on contract
- **Sticky feature** - Once adopted, becomes contract requirement
